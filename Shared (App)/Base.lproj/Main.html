Keyword Search

To enable the Safari extension, hit the “Aa”-button in the address bar and go to the “Manage Extensions” submenu.

Now try typing e.g. “y Rick Astley” in the Safari address bar to try a keyword search.

To edit your searches and settings, open the “Keyword Search” submenu under the “Aa”-menu.
